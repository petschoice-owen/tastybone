THE TASTYBONE

SIZE GUIDE

How to choose the best TastyBone chew toy for your pooch

SAFETY FIRST

At TastyBone we want to ensure your dog is chewing the right size bone for them. We recommend choosing based on their size rather than weight, as the size of your dog’s jaw determines which bone is the safest. Our bones come in toy, small, large and giant sizes.

CHOOSE YOUR DOG’S

CHEW SIZE

Always go for the bone which is larger than your dog’s jaw.
If you’re unsure which size to go for, we recommend sizing up for safety.

 

If you have more than one dog in your house,

pick a chew suitable for the
